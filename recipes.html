Charcuterie Board

Ingredients

    Meats:

  • Salchichon - a Spanish salame
  • Del Duca - a dry-cured ham
  • Chorizo Cantimpalo
  • Linguiça - a spiced pork sausage, similar to chorizo
  • Tinned fish

    • Cheeses:

  • Queso Mahón Menorca - a semi-soft cow's milk cheese
  • Zamorano - a hard sheep's milk cheese

    • Accompaniments:

  • Bread
  • Sweet Red Pepper Jam
  • Olives - Manzanilla and Taberna green olives
  • Green Olive Dip
Instructions
  1. Cook the linguiça on a grill or grill-pan over medium-high heat until nicely browned. Slice on the bias to serve.
  2. Arrange the meats and cheeses on a platter, and enjoy with fresh bread, tinned fish, red pepper jam, olives, and olive spread.

Gazpacho

Ingredients
  • Gazpacho

    • Garnishes:

  • Frozen tomato juice
  • Frozen cucumbers
Instructions
  1. Taste and season the gazpacho (likely agressively) with salt and pepper.
  2. Ladle soup into chilled bowls. Garnish with tomato ice cubes and cucumbers.

Mini Salt Cod Sandwiches

Ingredients
  • Salt Cod Mixture
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mini hamburger buns
  • Sweet Red Pepper Jam
  • Relish Mayo
Instructions

(Makes 4 mini-sandwiches)

  1. Heat 2 Tbsps of olive oil in a medium non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Working in batches, drop the cod batter by scant 1/4 cup-fuls into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through, 2-4 minutes per side. Take a nibble, there should be no floury taste. Transfer to paper towels to drain. Add more oil to the pan as needed.
  3. Spread the bottoms of the buns with relish mayo and layer each with a lettuce leaf, tomato slice, and a patty. Dollop with a bit of the red pepper jam and crown with the top of the bun.
