Meat & Cheese Board

Ingredients

    Meats:

  • Linguiça - a spiced pork sausage, similar to chorizo
  • Salchichon - a Spanish salame
  • Del Duca - a dry-cured ham
  • Chorizo Cantimpalo

    • Cheeses:

  • Queso Mahón Menorca - a semi-soft cow's milk cheese
  • Zamorano - a hard sheep's milk cheese

    • Accompaniments:

  • Bread
  • Red Pepper Jam
  • Olives - blank and blank green olives
  • Olive spread
Instructions
  1. Slice the Linguiça on the bias into quarter-inch slices, and cook on a grill or grill-pan over medium-high heat until nicely browned.
  2. Arrange the meats and cheeses on a platter, and enjoy with fresh bread, red pepper jam, and olive spread
